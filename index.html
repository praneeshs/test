Frequently Asked Questions About Chotupay

Find answers to common questions about our GST billing software and features

What is Chotupay and who should use it?

Chotupay is a cloud-based GST billing, POS, and accounting software designed specifically for Indian businesses. It's perfect for retail shops, e-commerce businesses, restaurants, service providers, and small to medium enterprises that need GST-compliant invoicing and inventory management.

Is there a free plan or trial available?

Yes! Chotupay offers a 30-day completely free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start. After the trial, you can upgrade to a paid plan or continue with our free starter plan with limited features.

Does Chotupay support e-invoicing and IRN generation?

Yes, absolutely! Chotupay provides automatic IRN generation for e-invoicing (IRP), QR code generation, and e-invoice submission to the GST portal. This is available on Professional and Enterprise plans and ensures 100% GST compliance.

How many modules does Chotupay have?

Chotupay includes 15+ integrated modules including: GST invoicing, POS system, inventory management, accounting, analytics, CRM, e-invoicing, reports, team collaboration, mobile app, barcode scanning, expense tracking, and more.

Is Chotupay safe and secure?

Yes, security is our top priority. Chotupay uses bank-level AES-256 encryption, SSL/TLS protocols, automatic daily backups, geo-redundancy, and complies with international security standards. We guarantee 99.9% uptime with automatic failover.

Can I access Chotupay on my mobile phone?

Yes! Chotupay is fully responsive and works on all devices. Plus, we offer dedicated iOS and Android mobile apps with offline mode support so you can bill customers anywhere, anytime.

What support options are available?

Chotupay offers 24/7 email and live chat support. Professional and Enterprise plans include phone support and priority responses. We also have comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and a knowledge base.

Can I export my data from Chotupay?

Yes, you can export invoices, reports, and customer information in PDF, Excel, and CSV formats anytime. We ensure complete data portability and have no lock-in contracts.
