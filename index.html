10 GST Compliance Tips for Indian Retailers

GST compliance is crucial for Indian retailers. In this guide, we'll share 10 essential tips to help you stay compliant, avoid penalties, and streamline your billing process.

1. Maintain Accurate Records

Keep detailed records of all invoices, receipts, and payments. Accurate record-keeping helps during audits.

2. File Returns On Time

Always file required returns before deadlines to avoid penalties.

3. Use GST-Compliant Invoicing Software

Use software like Chotupay that automates GST calculations and invoicing.

4. Verify Supplier GSTIN

Verify supplier GSTINs to ensure ITC eligibility and prevent fraud.

5. Reconcile Monthly

Identify discrepancies early by monthly reconciliation.
